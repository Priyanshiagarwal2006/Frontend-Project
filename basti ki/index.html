About Us

Basti Ki Pathshala Foundation is a registered nonprofit that addresses educational inequality in Indian slums by bringing free, quality teaching and holistic support to children. With a philosophy centered on collaboration, it provides structured learning, material support, and personal development opportunities. Interns and volunteers form a vital part of its ecosystem, contributing to awareness raising and direct impact work.
